String.toLowerCase toUpperCase should specify Locale.ENGLISH
There are quite a few instances of String.toLowerCase - and some of toUpperCase - method calls which don t specify the Locale. These should probably mostly all use Locale.ENGLISH otherwise there may be problems in some Locales. e.g. Turkey where i .toUpperCase is not equal to I - and vice-versa.
